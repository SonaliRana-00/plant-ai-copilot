SOP-001: Cooling Water Low Pressure
If cooling water supply pressure drops below 3.5 bar,
immediately check valve XV-89 position and compressor
C-02 outlet pressure. If pressure remains low after
5 minutes notify shift supervisor.

SOP-002: High Temperature Response
If TT-101 temperature exceeds 90 degrees C reduce
feed rate by 20 percent and increase cooling water
flow on FT-101. If temperature exceeds 96 degrees C
initiate emergency shutdown procedure ESP-04.

SOP-003: Low Flow Alarm
If FT-101 flow drops below 30 m3/hr check control
valve CV-101 position and upstream supply pressure.
Inspect strainer for blockage if valve is fully open.

SOP-004: High Pressure Response
If PT-101 pressure exceeds 8 bar open bypass valve
BV-101 immediately and notify shift supervisor.
Do not exceed 9 bar under any circumstances.

SOP-005: Heat Exchanger Fouling
Rising differential pressure across shell side
combined with reduced heat transfer indicates fouling.
Schedule inspection and mechanical cleaning within
48 hours. Log in SAP PM as preventive maintenance.
